India-Oz final T20I shifted to Bengaluru

The venues are set to be tweaked with the M Chinnaswamy stadium confirmed as a replacement venue for the final match on December 3. The contest was scheduled in Hyderabad but has been moved to Bengaluru as it is also the Telangana asse Telangana assembly election's vote counting day.

According to sources, Telangana police expressed their inability to provide security on the day. It is learnt that the BCCI did not want a repeat of the September 29 World Cup warm-up game between New Zealand and Pakistan, which was held behind closed doors. The move was necessitated after the Hyderabad city police expressed their inability to provide security owing to festivals.
